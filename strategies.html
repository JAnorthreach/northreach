Investment Strategies

Multiple strategies. One disciplined investment process.

NorthReach evaluates each opportunity based on market demand, capital requirements, execution risk and the intended exit or hold strategy.

01

Ground-Up Development

Acquisition and development of land for residential, townhome, commercial or industrial projects. NorthReach coordinates underwriting, design, engineering, permitting, budgeting, financing and development oversight through delivery.

Acquire → Design → Permit → Build → Sell or Stabilize
02

BRRRR

Acquisition of residential assets with improvement potential, followed by renovation, leasing and refinancing. The strategy is structured around basis control, stabilized value, long-term cash flow and capital recycling.

Buy → Rehab → Rent → Refinance → Repeat
03

Land Development

Identification and control of land, entitlement analysis, civil and utility coordination, subdivision or site planning, and preparation for vertical construction or disposition.

Identify → Entitle → Improve → Build or Sell
04

Value-Add Investments

Repositioning of underperforming residential, commercial or industrial assets through targeted capital improvements, operational adjustments and a defined leasing or disposition strategy.

Acquire → Improve → Reposition → Hold or Exit
